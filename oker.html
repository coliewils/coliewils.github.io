About Me

LoganWDev, is one of Illinois' most versatile VJs. Catch him in the scene and all over Chicago's hottest venues. His visual sets bring stimulating content and cutting-edge projection-mapping to Bar/Clubs/Venues, and anywhere in between. This versatility allows him to play to a variety of audiences as both a main Vj, or can even fill in last minute. Don't fret. LoganWDev have captivated audiences for many popular touring DJs as well as a headlining act in Chicago's most coveted nightclubs.

Born in the USA's East Coast, growing up in Main, and later, spending time in New Orleans, NeoCord was exposed to unique visions and styles often not found in the midwest or mainstream scenes. These influences have helped set NeoCord apart from his peers. From an early age, he was surrounded by a love for music, entertainment, & visual artistry, and swiftly developed a love for creating dope video accompaniments and sharing new discoveries with whomever he could. His adoration for these fundamentals that are the core of VJing has already taken him far in his growing career.
Neocord cares not only about the art of VJing, but also the overall entertainment of events his is a part of. In Chicago, he is the go-to VJ for the Good Music Collective party hosted every summer, and one of the group choice Vjs. NeoCord can regularly be found at his resident spots at several top nightclubs in the Chicago area.
